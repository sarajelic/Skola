About Us

Škola is an alternative rock/punk band from Zagreb, blending raw energy with unapologetic noise and chaos. The lineup — Maksimilijan Živković, Roko Gradski, Davor Grabovac, Eva Galić, and Iva Gradski — delivers a sound that’s loud, restless, and impossible to ignore. We’re here to shake things up, one distorted chord at a time.
